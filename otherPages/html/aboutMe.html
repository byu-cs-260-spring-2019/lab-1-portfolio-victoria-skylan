A look into my personal life Hobbies and Interests

ART

I love art. I enjoy photography and videography. My favorite thing to take pictures of the stars. It is such a surreal experience to let to the shutter be exposed to the world for a long period of time in order to capture the beauty that the human eye can't see.

 

TENNIS

I started playing tennis when I was a little girl, and I got serious with it when I was 14. At that point I spent all the time I could playing. I would go to group lessons, I would take private lessons, I would spend time practicing my serves and playing matches. Tennis was a way to challenge myself, a way to learn and grow. Check out Ivory Ridge Tennis Center

RAFTING

I love river rafting. I learned this love when I was little. My family went on a week long rafting trip on the Salmon rive and that was when I knew that I wanted to be a river guide when I grew up. This past summer I had the opprotunity to become a river guide and a photographer on the Salmon river. Check Out The Companies Instagram One of my favorite things to do was swim the rapids after I was done taking pictures of them. It was the quickest way to get from my photo spot to the boat.

EDUCATION

I love School, it is what I spend almost all of my time doing. For me, school is an oprotunity to challenge yourself, develop emotionally as well as intelligently, and a place to learn new perspectives about the world.
